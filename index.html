Finals Lab Task 6: MongoDB Practice

This portfolio demonstrates the use of MongoDB for document-oriented database operations using the Mongo shell. The task is divided into several parts: creating a database, inserting documents into collections, querying and updating those documents, and working with relationships across multiple collections.

STEP BY STEP PROCESS

STEP 1

Open terminal and connect to MongoDB instance.

STEP 2

Create and use a database named mongo_practice.

STEP 3

Insert the following documents into a movies collection using db.movies.insert().

  • Each document includes attributes such as title, writer, year, actors, and optionally franchise or synopsis.
  • Insert a variety of documents including: Fight Club, Pulp Fiction, Inglorious Basterds, The Hobbit series, Pee Wee Herman's Big Adventure, and Avatar.

STEP 4

Perform queries to retrieve documents from the movies collection.

  • Retrieve all documents.
  • Retrieve documents where the writer is "Quentin Tarantino".
  • Find documents with actors that include "Brad Pitt".
  • Retrieve all documents from the Hobbit franchise.
  • Query movies released in the 1990s or outside the 2000â€“2010 range.

STEP 5

Update existing documents in the movies collection.

  • Add missing synopsis fields.
  • Add a new actor to a movie.

STEP 6

Search text within the synopsis fields.

  • Use regular expressions to find documents with specific words or word combinations.

STEP 7

Delete specific movie documents.

  • Delete the movies "Pee Wee Herman's Big Adventure" and "Avatar".

STEP 8

Manage related data using collections users, posts, and comments.

  • Insert user profiles with nested documents.
  • Insert multiple blog posts assigned to users.
  • Insert related comments using the post ObjectId.

STEP 9

Perform relationship-based queries.

  • Query all users, posts, and comments.
  • Filter posts and comments by author.
  • Find all comments tied to a specific post.
